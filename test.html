          O MATO calls for another edition of last year's memorable blast!

          Only 45 min away from Berlin's urban jungle, we settled down at Insel Lindwerder, a scenic island surrounded by Wannsee’s dazzling waters and bordering forests.

          For 12 hours you will be able to delve into new soundscapes while being embraced by pure nature & sun. In combination with a range of sport activities and cultural performances, you’ll find yourself in utopia’s universe. A hedonistic playground for communities to exchange on a common base: music and subculture.

          Dancers will be welcomed on two floors with an exceptionally well curated blend of international as well as local artists. A diverse musical set-up incorporating various electronic genres on one floor, as well as organic styles such as brazilian, hip hop, world and jazz on the other.

          How to get back /

          This is a daytime festival, which is why the music will end at 10 pm. Please note, that the last public transfer leaves at 9 pm. We strongly advise you to come by bike or share a cab (approx. €2 p.P.) Furthermore, we will provide a bus service, shuttling from Insel Lindwerder to S-Bahn station Nikolasee.

          There are three different and limited bus tickets (€2 + RA fee):

          _ from 9pm to 10pm
          _ from 10pm to 11pm
          _ from 11pm to 12am

          As ferries from the island to the shuttle pick-up spot will take around 60 min, please make 100% sure, that you'll arrive at the particular bus in time.

          What is O MATO about?

          O MATO is a German/Brazilian collective creating a platform for natural and cultural values to be exchanged within a global community. We want to communicate environmental and socio-cultural issues in harmony with the desire for an alternative, hedonistic and adventurous lifestyle.
          The enhancement of cultural exchange is being realized throughout a series of events in Germany, Brazil and other countries around the world. Hereby we intend to promote tolerance and understanding on the basis of common interest: music and culture.

          Our main project is "The O MATO Experience", a new form of social gathering incorporating adventure travels, outdoor parties and indigenous traditions in the midst of the Brazilian Amazon (6th - 16th September). We enhance environmental awareness by offering local held workshops and nature explorations. Local staff, regional products and recycled materials are the essences for a sustainable treat with the local economy and nature.
